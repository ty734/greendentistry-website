A Dental Home Built Around Your Whole-Body Health

Most dental visits look at your teeth. We look at you. At Green Dentistry, Dr. Hye Park practices holistic, biological dentistry that connects your mouth to the rest of your health, using safer materials, gentler technology, and an unhurried approach designed around how you actually feel.

The Whole-Body Difference

We Care for the Whole You, Not Just Your Teeth

Your mouth is not separate from the rest of your body. It is the front door. What we put in it, and what we leave out of it, ripples through everything else, from how you sleep to how you heal.

That is the heart of biological dentistry. Instead of patching a problem and sending you on your way, Dr. Park looks for what is causing it in the first place. The goal is care that lasts, not another temporary fix.

You should never feel rushed or talked over. Here, you get time to ask questions, understand your options, and make choices that feel right for your body and your life.

Dr. Hye Park, DMD, has spent more than a decade building a practice around root-cause, whole-body dentistry. Patients describe her as a "walking holistic dental encyclopedia," and they drive from across Northern Virginia to see her.

Safer Materials

Dentistry Without Metal, Chosen to Work With Your Body

Many people come to us wondering what is actually in their mouth, and whether it belongs there. We share that thoughtfulness. We favor biocompatible, metal-free materials selected to reduce the risk of unwanted reactions.

If you have old silver fillings, Dr. Park removes them using the IAOMT's SMART protocol, with isolation, filtration, and extra protection designed to limit mercury vapor exposure during the process. For missing teeth, we offer metal-free ceramic implants.

The result is care that supports a beautiful, natural-looking smile you can feel good about, inside and out.

Advanced, Gentle Technology

Technology That Makes Care More Precise and Comfortable

The right technology does not make dentistry feel colder. Used well, it makes it gentler. We invest in tools that help us see more clearly, treat more conservatively, and keep you comfortable along the way.

That includes iTero digital scanning for impressions without the goop, CBCT 3D imaging for a fuller picture of your health, a dental microscope for microscopic bacteria evaluation, and Fotona laser therapy, including NightLase for airway and sleep support.

Saliva-based oral DNA testing and medical-grade ozone round out a toolkit designed around precision, comfort, and whole-body wellness.

Truly Green

Better for You, and Better for the Planet

The name is not just a name. Caring for your health and caring for the environment come from the same place, so we built sustainability into how the office runs day to day.

Digital x-rays mean less radiation and no chemical developer. Amalgam separators keep mercury out of the water supply. A multi-stage air filtration system helps keep the air clean, and a dry-air vacuum saves thousands of gallons of water compared with a conventional office.

You get high-quality, modern care without the environmental cost. For a lot of our patients, that peace of mind matters.
