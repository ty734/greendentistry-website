Technology in Service of Whole-Body Wellness

The right tools let us see more clearly, plan more precisely, and treat more gently. Every piece of technology here is chosen with one thing in mind: your comfort and your long-term health.

Why Our Technology Matters

Precise, Comfortable, Connected to Your Health

At Green Dentistry, we use advanced technology to make dentistry more precise, comfortable, and connected to whole-body wellness. Our technology helps us better understand each patient's unique needs, plan treatment with greater accuracy, and support a more conservative, minimally invasive approach whenever possible. Each piece of technology is selected with the patient experience in mind, from better diagnostics and digital planning to more comfortable care and support for long-term oral and overall health.

Digital Precision Advanced Diagnostics Minimally Invasive Healing Bacteria Reduction Custom Digital Dentistry

01 · Digital Precision

Impressions Without the Mess

Digital scanning replaces goopy molds with a fast, comfortable scan, giving us highly accurate models for planning and restorations.

iTero Digital Scanner

Our iTero scanner creates detailed 3D digital impressions without messy molds, so we can diagnose, monitor, and plan your treatment with greater precision and comfort.

  • No goopy impression trays
  • Highly accurate digital models
  • Better-fitting crowns, aligners, and night guards

02 · Advanced Diagnostics

Seeing the Full Picture

Better information leads to better, more conservative care. These tools help us understand what is happening beneath the surface before we ever begin treatment.

CBCT / 3D Imaging

3D cone beam imaging gives us a detailed, three-dimensional view of teeth, roots, bone, and airway, supporting more accurate diagnosis and careful treatment planning.

  • A complete 3D view, not just a flat X-ray
  • Supports implant, airway, and surgical planning
  • Low-radiation digital imaging

Microscopic Bacteria Evaluation

Our dental microscope lets us look closely at the bacteria and areas of concern in your mouth, supporting a more precise, biologically focused approach to your care.

  • A closer look at oral bacteria
  • Supports targeted, conservative treatment
  • Helps preserve healthy tissue

03 · Minimally Invasive Healing

Gentle Tools That Work With Your Body

Whenever possible, we choose approaches that are gentler on healthy tissue and supportive of your body's own ability to heal.

Fotona Laser

Our Fotona laser supports minimally invasive, comfortable care across a range of treatments, including NightLase therapy designed to support better breathing and more restful sleep.

  • Minimally invasive treatment options
  • NightLase support for airway and sleep
  • Designed for comfort and gentle healing

Ozone Therapy

Medical-grade ozone is a natural way to reduce harmful bacteria and support healing, with fewer chemicals along the way.

  • Naturally derived from oxygen
  • Designed to reduce harmful bacteria
  • Supports comfortable healing
Learn more about ozone →

04 · Bacteria Reduction & Hygiene Support

Healthier Gums, the Gentle Way

Pairing laser and ozone gives us gentle, effective ways to reduce harmful bacteria and support healthier gums, often with less need for harsher options.

Laser & Ozone Bacteria Reduction Therapy

Together, laser and medical-grade ozone help us reduce the bacteria associated with gum inflammation and support your hygiene care, gently and with healthy tissue in mind.

  • Designed to reduce harmful bacteria
  • Supports healthier gums and comfortable cleanings
  • May reduce reliance on harsher options

05 · Custom Digital Dentistry

Made Precisely for You

Digital design and in-office printing let us create custom appliances with precision and a faster, more personalized turnaround.

SprintRay 3D Printing

Our in-office SprintRay 3D printing lets us digitally design and create custom appliances with precision and efficiency, from night guards and retainers to surgical guides.

  • Custom-made, in-house precision
  • Faster turnaround for your appliances
  • A more personalized fit

Custom Oral Appliances & Models

From night guards to airway and sleep appliances, we design custom oral appliances built around your unique anatomy for comfort and a precise fit.

  • Designed around your anatomy
  • Supports comfort, airway, and protection
  • Crafted with biocompatible materials
