Pediatric Holistic Dentistry · Alexandria, VA

A Gentle, Whole-Body Start for Your Child's Smile

Children are not small adults, and their dental care should not be either. At Green Dentistry, we care for growing smiles with a gentle, whole-body approach, helping your child feel safe in the chair while we support how they grow, breathe, and thrive.

More Than Teeth

Caring for the Whole Child, Not Just the Smile

Your child's mouth is the gateway to their overall health, and we treat it that way. A first dental home is about so much more than counting teeth. It is where healthy habits, comfortable visits, and lifelong confidence begin.

Our holistic approach looks beyond cavities to support the way your child sleeps, breathes, speaks, and grows. By paying attention early, we can help guide healthy development rather than simply reacting to problems later.

And because every child is different, every visit is personalized to their age, their stage, and their personality.

Safe, Healthy, Kid-Friendly

What Holistic Kids' Care Looks Like

What goes into your child's mouth matters as much as what comes out. Our pediatric care is designed around safer materials and gentle, whole-body habits.

Gentle First Visits

Calm, no-pressure introductions to the dentist, explained in kid-friendly ways and paced to your child's comfort.

Fluoride-Alternative Care

Preventive options and BPA-free sealants designed to protect young teeth, with gentler alternatives to traditional fluoride.

Metal-Free Materials

Tooth-colored, biocompatible fillings and restorations, with no mercury and no metal by design.

Breathing & Growth Aware

We keep an eye on airway, breathing, tongue posture, and jaw development, and guide you if anything needs attention.

Low-Radiation Imaging

When imaging is needed, we use low-dose digital X-rays designed to keep exposure to a minimum.

A Calm Environment

A warm, welcoming office and a team that treats your child like family, so visits feel safe and even fun.

Why It Matters Early

Healthy Habits That Last a Lifetime

The early years shape so much of a child's long-term health. Watching how a child breathes, how their jaw develops, and how their teeth come in can reveal small things worth supporting before they become bigger ones.

Our goal is not to scare, but to empower. When you understand what to look for and your child feels comfortable at the dentist, you both walk in with confidence instead of worry.

We partner with you every step of the way, answering questions and helping you make thoughtful choices for your family.

One Dental Home

Care for the Whole Family, Under One Roof

Bringing the whole family to one trusted practice makes life simpler, and helps everyone build the same healthy habits together. From your child's first checkup to your own holistic care, we are here for all of it.

If your little one feels nervous, we go at their pace. If you have questions about breathing, habits, or what is normal at each age, we are glad to talk it through. This is dentistry that supports your child's whole body, not just their smile.
