Her Approach

A Dentist Who Treats the Whole Person

Dr. Park believes your mouth is not separate from the rest of your body. It is the front door. What goes into it, and what comes out of it, can ripple through your overall health. That belief shapes everything about how she practices.

Rather than only patching symptoms, she looks for the root cause of what is going on, so the same problems stop coming back. She takes the time to explain what is happening and why it matters, in plain language, and she keeps you in control of every decision along the way.

For Dr. Park, holistic care is not a buzzword. It is a commitment to safer materials, gentler visits, and a complete picture of your health, all under one roof.

Safer, Greener Dentistry

Care Designed Around Your Body and the Environment

Dr. Park is a certified SMART (Safe Mercury Amalgam Removal Technique) provider, following strict protocols designed to protect you, the team, and the environment during amalgam removal.

She is also committed to eco-friendly dentistry, using thoughtful systems such as green dental imaging, air and water purification, and a green infection-control system. Tools like medical-grade ozone, 3D imaging, and oral DNA testing help make visits gentler and more thorough.

The goal is simple: comprehensive, whole-body care that aligns with the values you already bring to your food, your home, and your family.
