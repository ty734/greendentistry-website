Meet the Green Dentistry Team

From the moment you walk in, you are met by people who genuinely care. Our compassionate, eco-conscious team is here to make every visit feel calm, welcoming, and centered on your comfort, safety, and long-term health.

Friendly Faces

Get to Know Our Team

Wazhma

Office Manager

Wazhma brings over a decade of experience in dentistry to her role as Office Manager. With a heart for people and a talent for organization, she is dedicated to supporting the practice's vision of creating positive, lasting changes in patients' lives. Her goal is to make every patient feel cared for, informed, and confident throughout their journey with us.

Outside of work, Wazhma is a proud mom of two. She loves traveling, exploring new adventures, and creating memories with her family.

Mary

Patient Advocate

Mary is the smiling face that greets every patient who visits our office. As our Patient Advocate, she has a unique talent for helping anxious patients feel at ease from the very first interaction. Her caring presence and compassionate approach reflect our commitment to creating a supportive, welcoming environment.

Outside the office, Mary is a proud mom of two. She loves exploring new experiences and cultures with her children, always finding ways to grow and learn together as a family.

Why It Feels Different Here

A Team That Treats You Like a Whole Person

Holistic care is not just what Dr. Park practices. It is how our whole team shows up for you. We take the time to listen, to explain, and to make sure you never feel like just another appointment on the schedule.

From safer, eco-conscious materials to the small comforts that make a visit easier, every detail is chosen with your wellbeing in mind. We want you to leave feeling informed, cared for, and genuinely looked after.
